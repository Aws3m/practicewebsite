About me Projects
My name is Sem Radoes. In 2013 I befriended people who work in IT. I was involved in their hobby projects and a wide world opened before my eyes. My education at that moment prevented me from studying ICT. Throughout my career as pedagogue, i realised this is not a job that would make me happy. It wasn't inline with my interests. When the opportunity arose, I started a bachelors degree. Soon i realised that an overall ICT degree wasn't what i was interested in. I wanted to be a developer for web apps and websites. I was interested the most in the front end. So i enrolled in a front end developer course and now i am an enthousiastic front end developer who works as a freelancer.
