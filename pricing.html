My work ethics

Responsive

Your website should respond to any screen size it's displayed on, whether it will be a laptop, tablet or phone, your website should look good. I make it happen, that's a promise!

Mobile First

80% of your website visits are phone users. So it comes naturally that your website is developed with that in mind. So we make it look good on your phone first, and move on from there to a tablet and laptop screen.

Cyber Security

The world wide web has been plagued with viruses and malware. Your website will be secure and the information you recieve from email will be subject to extreme caution to GDPR rules and safety measurements
